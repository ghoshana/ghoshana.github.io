Research Focus

I develop multi-agent decision architectures for distributed network resource allocation, integrating reinforcement learning, safety-constrained control, and systems-level modeling to enable resilient, interpretable, and energy-aware network management in 5G/6G and SD-WAN environments.

Multi-Agent RL Safe RL / CBF SDN/SD-WAN UAV Networks 5G/6G Slicing QoS Provisioning

My work emphasizes reproducibility, interpretability, and measurable impact on real-world QoS metrics. I build end-to-end prototypes: simulators, controllers, learning pipelines, and evaluation frameworks.

NS-3 Simulation PyTorch / TensorFlow OpenAI Gym 3GPP Standards
