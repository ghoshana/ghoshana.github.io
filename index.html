Research Engineer

I'm a Research Engineer at LEAT – Université Côte d'Azur, Sophia Antipolis, France.

Initiative-taking engineering researcher with industrial experience in system design, integration and implementation. PhD in Software Asset Management and NFV from ENS de Lyon / Inria (Feb 2023). Current research: SDN-WAN + UAV + Multi-Agent RL for tiered-QoS 5G slicing.

About

Research Interests

My research combines SDN-WAN architecture, UAV-assisted networks, and multi-agent reinforcement learning to solve problems in 5G network slicing and differentiated QoS provisioning. I work on resource allocation optimization and ML-based performance modeling for next-generation wireless networks.
